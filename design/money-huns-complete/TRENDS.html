TRENDS IN GLOBAL FUNDS MANAGEMENT

Indian Association of Investment Professionals (IAIP), is pleased to invite you to an event on Global Best Practices and Current Trends for the Alternative Asset Industry. The session will be presented by Chris Franzek, Managing Director at Duff & Phelps, New York Office.

Chris will cover reporting and valuation processes for PE and VC funds globally and also look at India-specific complexities. He will speak on equity, and also cover debt and mezzanine financing which are emerging as the new investment route into Indian companies.
