GeoBats Truetype Font for Windows (version 1)

2007 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbat font with over 130 glyph symbols of geographic shapes - continents, regions, countries, US states, Canadian provinces, etc.

Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
