Who is this site for?

Money Management India is relevant for professionals in the Indian financial services industry who are involved in retail or institutional savings. It also serves as a central resource for overseas investors investing in India.

In particular, we hope to serve –

  1. Investment advisers or portfolio construction practitioners - those who design, build and/or manage investment portfolios (or who must understand how others do so)

  2. Wealth managers, distributers brokers and agents–those who advise or sell to individual or institutional investors on wealth management issues, including investments and wealth planning (such as mutual fund distributers, insurance agents and brokers, stock brokers, real estate agents and brokers etc)

  3. Ancillary advisers– those who advise individual or institutional investors on other issues, such as tax and legal, which affect investments (such as accountants, lawyers, media advisers, academia etc)

  4. Overseas investors investing in India– those who advise, or are themselves, individual or institutional investors on investing into India (such as investment consolting, pension funds, family offices, or any of the above categories)

  5. Product manufacturers– those who offer financial products to any of the above (such as asset management companies, alternative investment funds, insurance companies, investment banks etc)

What we do

We aim to increase investors’ financial well-being by promoting better design of investment products, better delivery of wealth management advice and better communication. We do this through sharing, discussion and debate.

We invite industry professionals to participate through

  • experts sharing knowledge, opinions and battle scars through short articles or videos,

  • commenting on others’ content,

  • starting or commenting on important topical issues,

  • presenting and attending events.

We do not promote specific products or securities, nor comment on short term views on markets. We focus on investment strategies and ideas with a medium to long term market horizon.

We will partner with relevant industry associations and sponsors who share our commitment to research, education and knowledge exchange. Their support enables us to cover costs and publish the website without charge to its readers. We retain the site’s content and editorial independence at all times.

Who we are

The Money Management India network is an initiative by Hansipra Solutions Private Limited (previously known as Hubbis Consulting Private Limited). Its principal is Hansi Mehrotra. You can read her profile at https://www.linkedin.com/in/hansimehrotra

About Hansi Mehrotra

Hansi has 20 years of financial services industry experience, primarily in investment research and consulting for the wealth management industry.

Most recently, Hansi formed a joint venture to set up the India office for Hubbis in 2012, a HK-based wealth management portal and professional development firm for the Asian wealth management industry. She wrote and published articles, guides, educational content, as well as organised a number of industry events.

Before becoming an entrepreneur, Hansi was Asia Pacific head of wealth management for Mercer’s Investment Consulting business, based in Singapore. She was responsible for the firm's investment offering to private banks and wealth management firms, having started this division in Australia in 2003. She also launched Mercer's investment business in India in 2008, and led a number of projects including design of the investment options for the New Pension System. Before Mercer, she held a similar role at van Eyk Research, a leading research firm in Australia. In addition to content, Hansi’s roles have included the development of the online service delivery to financial advisers.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from Delhi University, Graduate Diploma of Applied Finance and Investments and Chartered Financial Analyst charter from CFA Institute.
